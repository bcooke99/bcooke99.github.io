Cocktail of the Month

Thalia

A riff on a water lily, this short drink is sweet, light, and fruity!

"I think it's pretty neat" - Ben Cooke

Ingredients

  • 1oz gin
  • 0.75oz cointreau
  • 0.5oz creme de violette
  • 0.5oz elderflower liqueur
  • 2 dashes orange bitters

1. Chill your glassware, I like to use a tumbler

2. Assemble ingredients

3. Measure and pour your ingredients into a mixing glass with ice

4. Stir for 30-45 seconds

5. Pour over a big ice cube into your chilled glass

6. Enjoy!

Author: Ben Cooke
